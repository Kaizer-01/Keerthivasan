About

Technology Consultant with a Master's in Management and an undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering. With over 3 years of experience, I've earned a reputation for excellence in consulting with multinational firms.

I've showcased strong leadership skills by successfully managing teams of 20+ consultants and developers. My expertise lies in delivering exceptional results for clients in industries such as automobile, insurance, and healthcare. I specialize in process consulting, change management, and implementing advanced technology solutions. Working with clients worldwide has given me a deep understanding of international business practices and the ability to foster cross-cultural partnerships.

Beyond consulting, I actively engage in sustainable development initiatives, collaborating with diverse stakeholders and making significant contributions to global causes. I participate in conferences and simulations, particularly those hosted or partnered by the United Nations. Outside of work, I pursue freelance design and photography, collaborating with studios of various sizes. I've also founded my own digital design studio, where I constantly strive to introduce innovative ideas. Leveraging my multifaceted skill set and unwavering commitment, I dedicate my time to managing and consulting NGOs and NPOs, aiming to make a positive impact on the world.
